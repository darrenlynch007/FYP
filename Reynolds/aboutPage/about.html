About us

our location

We are located in the heart of the north west of Ireland in Letterkenny, co. Donegal

Creative music for creative people

REYNOLDS MUSIC have available the best trained team of retail professionals in our industry. We want you to feel comfortable and confident in all your dealings with us, particularly if you're just beginning to explore the joys of music-making and need help and advice. Our friendly staff look forward to welcoming you.

In addition to the obvious music products, Reynolds Music are able to supply all manner of electrical goods from domestic appliances to professional lighting, special effects, staging and much more. We also have an installations team who can take on projects of any size, including fully-fledged nightclub installations.

our products

All the products in our range are selected for their quality, performance, and value for money. In the rare event that they fail we offer a full 12 month warranty on all products (excluding consumables)
